AFFORDA Logistics

Philosophy

An integral part of AFFORDA, it is considered as the backbone of its growing business. It moves AFFORDA products from one place to another in the service of its valued clients and customers, and all AFFORDA's logistical needs as required. However, it has its own mechanics of generating income to ensure maintenance funding, upkeep and integrity of its logistical fleet.

Provisions

  1. Internal requirement: Defined as moving/transporting AFFORDA products as an added service to its clients and customers wherein corresponding logistic fee will be "collected internally" for the logistical support provided of which the amount is commensurate with the product load and distance covered.
  2. External requirement: Defined as moving/transporting AFFORDA products at the request of its clients and customers wherein corresponding logistic fee will be "collected from the client/customer" for the logistical support provided of which the amount is commensurate with the product load and distance covered.
  3. AFFORDA Balloons and Deco Service requirement: Defined as "internal requirement" of which "top priority privilege" is always given to avail logistical support wherein provision number 1 applies
