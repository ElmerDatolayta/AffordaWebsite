Afforda Frozen Delight Franchising


As part of the company's expansion program, AFFORDA supports franchising. It is open not only to fresh entrepreneurs but also to long time and existing business owners who wanted to expand their business scopes by catering AFFORDA Frozen Delights products.


We recommend that our prospective franchisees shall call us or visit our home office from more details in moving forward.


Basic requirements and obligations


  1. This requires joint occular inspection between franchisee and AFFORDA representative to initially approve the shop or plant location

  2. The franchise has the option to expand thru open distributorship and dealership/retail to qualified parties

  3. Under no cirscumstances a franchisee can expand his franchise apart from distributorship and dealership without afford's consent and approval.
